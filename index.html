About Me

My Journey

Hey there! I'm Navid, a German international student who moved to the US in 2017 with a soccer ball and big dreams. I had an incredible journey as a college soccer player, eventually becoming team captain and earning All-American honors. The highlight was playing in a national final before hanging up my boots to pursue coaching at both college and club levels.

After completing my undergraduate and master's degrees in business finance, I discovered my passion for data science. I spent over two years as a Data Analyst Consultant working with Fortune 500 clients in the financial sector, specializing in A/B testing, experimentation, and data-driven decision making. Currently, I'm pursuing another master's in Computer Science to deepen my technical expertise.
